## Scheduling Rules

### Priority Order
1. **Sales leads** — schedule within 48 hours, even if 2-3 weeks out. Other things move for these.
2. **YPO** — 24-hour email response. Events not moveable (80% attendance required). REX is high priority but can miss if business-necessary.
3. **UTB Board** — not moveable, in-person expected. Block travel time + 1 hour overrun after.
4. **One-on-ones** — do not move unless absolutely necessary. In-person except Rod, Mark & Vicki.
5. **Family** — not moveable once booked unless significant need, check with David first.

### Categories
- Sales, Work, Relationship, Personal, Proactive, YPO, UTB Board, Family, Travel

### Preferences
- Less than 3 video calls/day (convert to phone when possible)
- Schedule 25 or 55 minutes for Teams calls (watch back-to-back)
- In-person preferred for networking & sales leads
- Networking: coffee > lunch. 30 minutes default.
- Appointments (providers): early morning, late afternoon, or over lunch
- Interviews: 30 min + 30 min buffer after
- "Blocked" time = reservations, can be used by sales team or David for sales activity
- Confirm meetings the morning of with participants. If they cancel, text David directly.

## Follow-Up Process (conferences, events, meetings)

1. Find and connect on LinkedIn with congenial message referencing the event
2. Enter contact in CRM — tag event, include David's notes
3. If email exists, draft follow-up email (happy to have met them + space for David's additional notes)
4. If no response, follow up 2 weeks later
5. Goal: in-person follow-up if in priority areas (DFW, STL, OKC, NYC)

- YPO = 24-hour response
