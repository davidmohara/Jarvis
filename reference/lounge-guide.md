# Airport Lounge Playbook — David O'Hara

**Last Updated**: February 19, 2026

## Your Access Cards

| Card | Lounge Access | Guest Policy |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| **Amex Platinum** | Centurion Lounges, Priority Pass Select (1,800+ lounges), Delta Sky Clubs (10 visits/yr), Plaza Premium, Escape Lounges | Centurion: guests $50/ea (free 2 guests if $75K+ annual spend). Priority Pass: 2 free guests. |
| **Citi AAdvantage Executive** | All Admirals Clubs (~50 locations), Oneworld partner lounges, Alaska Lounges | 2 free guests OR immediate family. Full membership benefit — no day-of-flight class restriction. |

**Bottom line**: Between these two cards, you're covered at virtually every airport you'll fly through. No need to pay for anything.

---

## Your Priority Order

1. **Centurion Lounge** — Best food, drinks, atmosphere. Access up to 3 hours before departure.
2. **Admirals Club** — Your home base on AA. Everywhere AA flies, there's usually one. No time restriction.
3. **Priority Pass lounge** — Backup when neither of the above is available or convenient.

---

## DFW — Home Base (Terminal D for parking, all AA terminals A-E)

You always park at Terminal D. AA flies out of Terminals A, B, C, D (international), and occasionally E. Skylink connects all terminals in ~5 minutes.

| Lounge | Location | Access Via | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Centurion Lounge** | Terminal D, mezzanine opposite Gate D12 | Amex Platinum | **First choice.** Right where you park. Craft cocktails, chef-driven food. |
| Admirals Club | Terminal A, across from Gate A24 | Citi Executive | Recently renovated. Good if departing from A gates. |
| Admirals Club | Terminal B, between Gates B3-B4 | Citi Executive | Smaller. Regional flights. |
| Admirals Club | Terminal C, between Gates C19-C20 | Citi Executive | Opens earliest (4 AM). Good for early flights from C. |
| Admirals Club | Terminal D, near Gate D24 | Citi Executive | Large. Showers. Close to Centurion — use if Centurion is packed. |
| Admirals Club | Terminal E, mezzanine | Citi Executive | Newest, nicest AC at DFW. Completed 2020. |
| The Club DFW | Terminal D | Priority Pass | Backup if Centurion has a wait. Buffet food + bar. |
| Plaza Premium | Terminal E, near Gate E31 | Priority Pass | Solid backup. 6 AM–8 PM. |

**DFW Play**: Park Terminal D → Centurion Lounge → Skylink to your gate. If you're departing from Terminal E, the E Admirals Club is worth checking out — it's the newest.

## Houston IAH — Robyn/Stephen visits

AA operates from **Terminal A**. No Centurion Lounge at IAH (the Centurion is in Terminal D, which is United territory).

| Lounge | Location | Access Via | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Centurion Lounge** | Terminal D | Amex Platinum | Requires terminal change from AA's Terminal A. Inter-terminal train available but adds 10-15 min. Worth it if you have time. |
| **Admirals Club** | Terminal A | Citi Executive | **Most convenient.** Right in the AA terminal. Solid option. |
| Priority Pass options | Various | Priority Pass | The Club at IAH (Terminal E), KLM Crown Lounge (Terminal D) |

**IAH Play**: If you have 2+ hours, take the train to Terminal D for Centurion. Otherwise, Admirals Club in Terminal A — zero hassle.

## Austin AUS — SXSW, regional business

Single terminal (Barbara Jordan). AA operates near Gates 9-22.

| Lounge | Location | Access Via | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Admirals Club** | Near Gate 22, mezzanine level | Citi Executive | **Your only real option.** Functional. New expanded club (~11,575 sq ft + outdoor terrace) coming 2027. |

**AUS Play**: Admirals Club. No Centurion here (yet — rumored for 2026-2027 expansion). Priority Pass only gets you a Jabbrrbox pod, which isn't worth it.

## Atlanta ATL — FABCON, connecting flights

AA operates from **Concourse T** (South Terminal). Centurion is in Concourse E (International Terminal).

| Lounge | Location | Access Via | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Centurion Lounge** | Concourse E, near Gate E11 | Amex Platinum | Largest Centurion in the network (26,000 sq ft). Outdoor terraces. Whiskey bar. Chef Deborah VanTrece menu. **BUT**: 20-30 min walk from AA gates in Concourse T. Can get very crowded with waitlists. |
| **Admirals Club** | Concourse T | Citi Executive | Right at your gate. Convenient. |

**ATL Play**: If you have 2+ hours before departure, the Centurion is spectacular — take the Plane Train from T to E. But it's frequently at capacity with a waitlist. If you're tight on time, stay in Concourse T at the Admirals Club.

## Las Vegas LAS — Sphere trip, leisure

AA flies from **Terminal 1** (Gates in Concourse D accessible via Blue Line tram).

| Lounge | Location | Access Via | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Centurion Lounge** | Concourse D, opposite Gate D1 | Amex Platinum | **First choice.** 175+ seats. 5 AM–11 PM. AA flights depart from D gates, so this is right there. |
| **Sidecar by Centurion** | Concourse D (opening 2026) | Amex Platinum | New speakeasy-style quick lounge. 90-min max stay. Good for tight connections. |
| Admirals Club | Terminal 1 | Citi Executive | Standard AC. |

**LAS Play**: Centurion, no question. You're already in Concourse D for AA flights.

## Rules to Remember

- **Centurion**: Access up to 3 hours before departure only. No arrival access. Effective July 2026, guests must be on the same flight.
- **Admirals Club**: Same-day boarding pass on AA or Oneworld required. No time restriction. 2 guests or immediate family free.
- **Priority Pass**: Enrollment required (do it through Amex if you haven't). 2 free guests per visit.
- **Alaska Lounges**: Accessible via Citi Executive card at LAX, PDX, SFO, SEA — good alternatives.
