# Kare Devices — Project Context

## Company
- **Product**: Kare Patch — adhesive tubing management sleeve for medical tubing (catheters, IV lines, feeding tubes, PICC lines, ports)
- **Site**: karedevices.com (Next.js, Fly.io, Stripe checkout)
- **Status**: Live and selling
- **Codebase**: ~/develop/karedevices/site2

## Product Details
- Medical-grade hypoallergenic adhesive, lasts up to 10 days
- Waterproof, shower-safe
- Compatible with 5-15mm tubing diameter
- Available in 5-packs and 20-packs
- Patented design, co-designed with healthcare professionals

## Target Audiences
1. **Patients & Home Caregivers** (age 30-80+) — want secure tubing, independence, reduced skin irritation
2. **Healthcare Providers** (nurses, clinical staff, 25-55) — want fewer dislodgements, less nursing time, patient comfort

## Brand Voice
- Empathetic, not sentimental
- Knowledgeable, not clinical
- Reliable, not stiff
- Empowering, not patronizing
- Never use fear-based messaging, urgency/scarcity tactics, or unsubstantiated clinical claims
