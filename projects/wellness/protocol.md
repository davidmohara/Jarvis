## IV. Nutrient & Safety Guardrails

- **Folate/B12 Management**: 1 capsule Thorne Basic B-Complex + AG1 = ~1,014mcg Folate daily. This is the optimal ceiling to avoid masking B12 issues while supporting methylation demands of peptide stack.
- **NAD+ Synergy**: Three-angle approach — direct NAD+, NR precursor, and Resveratrol for Sirtuin activation.
- **Hormone Balance**: With 70IU Testosterone weekly + 50mg DHEA daily, monitor for elevated estrogen symptoms (water retention, mood shifts). NAC at 1,300mg/day provides liver support during multi-compound cycle.
- **Creatine**: Reduced from 20g to 10g daily. Maintains muscle saturation with better GI tolerance and shake texture.

- **Inflammation**: hs-CRP, Homocysteine
