## I. Disposition — Cremation vs. Burial

### Decision: TBD

| Option | Key Considerations |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Traditional Burial** | Casket, cemetery plot, headstone. Gives family a physical place to visit. Higher cost ($7K-$15K+ in DFW). Requires cemetery selection. |
| **Cremation** | Simpler logistics. Ashes can be scattered, kept, or interred. Lower cost ($2K-$6K in DFW). Cremation rate in Texas ~45% and rising. Compatible with a full funeral service before or memorial after. |
| **Green/Natural Burial** | Biodegradable casket or shroud, no embalming. Limited cemetery options in DFW but growing. Aligns with stewardship values. |
| **Hybrid** | Cremation with urn burial in a cemetery plot — physical location for family + lower cost. |
