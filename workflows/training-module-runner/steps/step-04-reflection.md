# Step 4: Reflection

**Goal:** Brief reflection to lock in learning and capture user insights.

## Process

### 4.1 Ask Reflection Questions

Use the reflection questions from the guided walkthrough file. Keep it to 1-2 questions max. Don't turn this into an interview.

If the walkthrough doesn't have specific questions, use:
- "What was the most useful part of that for you?"
- "Anything you'd change about how that worked?"

### 4.2 Listen and Note

Capture their answer — it informs:
- Mastery notes (what they found valuable or struggled with)
- Future recommendations (if they loved delegation tracking, suggest follow-up nudges next)
- System customization opportunities (if they want a different format, note it for personal block updates)

### 4.3 Keep It Brief

One exchange. Don't belabor the reflection. If they give a short answer, that's fine — move on.
