## The Trust Evolution: 8 Stages of AI Maturity (Summary)

Improving's proprietary AI Adoption Model maps how organizations progress from zero AI usage to full agentic operations. The core insight: **as trust increases, humans shift from doing to directing.**

### Three Waves

| Wave | Stages | Focus |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Wave 1: Tools** | 1-2 | Efficiency gains from off-the-shelf AI tools |
| **Wave 2: Process** | 3-4 | Structured engineering creates repeatable task and workflow automation |
| **Wave 3: Agentic Operations** | 5-8 | Productivity multiplies as humans shift from doing to directing |

### The 8 Stages

| Stage | Name | Human Role | What It Looks Like |
|-------|------|------------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Zero AI** | N/A | No AI tools in use. Manual processes throughout. |
| 2 | **Off the Shelf** | Doer with an advisor | Using ChatGPT, Copilot, etc. without structured workflows. AI advises, human does all work. |
| 3 | **Task** | Reviewer of task output | AI executes a single defined task independently. Human reviews. *First Autonomy Inflection Point.* |
| 4 | **Workflow** | Reviewer of workflow output | AI coordinates multiple tasks across a workflow. Governance, quality gates in place. |
| 5 | **Delegate** | Delegator | Agents move outside primary tools. You receive status and output only. *Second trust boundary.* |
| 6 | **Coordinate** | Coordinator | Multiple agents work simultaneously. One human managing a team of agents. |
| 7 | **Supervise** | Goal-setter | AI orchestrator assigns work, manages pipeline, escalates to humans for decisions only. Currently at the frontier. |
| 8 | **Orchestrate** | Strategic director | End-to-end with minimal human intervention. |

### Key Concepts to Drop in Conversation

- **The Autonomy Inflection Point (Stage 2 to 3):** The jump from tools to task agents means letting AI do work you'll be held accountable for. Tooling alone yields 18-25% gains. Without embedded coaching, fewer than 45% of practitioners cross this threshold.
- **The Trust Boundary (Stage 4 to 5):** Moving agents outside your IDE means you can't see the work happening. Requires trust built through successful Stage 3-4 execution.
- **Most companies are at Stage 2.** That's not a dig - it's just where the industry is. The progression is intentional. You can't skip stages.
- **Not every team needs Stage 7 or 8.** Safety-critical work (like construction!) may target Stage 4-5 as the right ceiling.

### For Construction Specifically

Frame it around their world: document management, project coordination, field reporting, safety compliance, scheduling, RFIs, submittals. Every one of those is a workflow with boxes and arrows. The model helps them see which boxes AI can own vs. which need a human.

## AI Deep Learning Program (Brief Intro)

If the maturity model conversation lands and they ask "how do we actually move our people forward?" - this is the answer.

### What It Is

A **12-week embedded coaching program** that moves teams from Stage 2 to Stage 4 on the maturity model. Not a lecture series - it's hands-on with a dedicated AI coach working alongside their team daily.

### Structure

- **12 weeks** | 6 half-day workshops (every other week)
- **Phase 1 (Weeks 1-6):** Foundations - prompt engineering, AI-assisted workflows, coding with AI, TDD. Crosses the Autonomy Inflection Point.
- **Phase 2 (Weeks 7-12):** Advanced - agentic workflows, MCP integration, automation pipelines, governance agents. Teams become reviewers directing AI output.

### Delivery Model

- **Track Lead:** Senior trainer-coach who delivers workshops and coordinates across the team
- **Embedded AI Engineer:** A dedicated coach working alongside the team daily - building habits, applying concepts in real work, extracting reusable workflows

### What They Walk Away With

- Prompt engineering mastery
- AI assistants integrated into their actual workflows
- Ability to build and chain agents for autonomous task execution (Stage 3)
- Cross-system workflow automation with reusable skills (Stage 4)
- Governance habits for safe autonomous operations
- A 90-day framework to sustain maturity growth toward Stage 5+

### The Pitch Line

"Tooling alone gets you 18-25% efficiency. The Deep Learning Program gets you past the inflection point where productivity actually multiplies - with the coaching and governance to do it safely."

### Starts With

A **Readiness Assessment** to tailor the program to their team's stack, tools, and goals. Low-commitment first step.
