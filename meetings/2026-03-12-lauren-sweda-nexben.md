## Discovery Questions

### Understanding the Tool
1. "Walk me through the pricing analysis process today — is this manual, spreadsheet-based, or partially automated?"
2. "Who are the primary users of this tool? Brokers? Internal analysts? Employers?"
3. "What data sources feed into the pricing analysis? Is it pulling from your platform, external carriers, government data?"
4. "What does 'good' look like? Is there a competitive tool you're benchmarking against?"

### Understanding the Engagement
5. "Do you have a technical team internally, or would this be fully outsourced development?"
6. "What's driving the timeline? Is there a regulatory deadline, sales need, or competitive pressure?"
7. "Have you scoped this with anyone else, or are we your first conversation?"
8. "What's the decision-making process? Who else would be involved in selecting a partner?"

### Qualifying

10. "Would you prefer a fixed-scope engagement or an ongoing development partnership?"
