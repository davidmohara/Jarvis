# Fortium Partners Symposium — 5-Minute Talk

**Date:** Thursday, March 26, 2026 | 12:25–12:30 PM
**Location:** OMNI Hotel Dallas
**Format:** Lunch sponsor intro — 5 minutes before meal

---

## Audience

Fortium Partners' network of 180+ fractional/interim CTOs, CIOs, and CISOs. 15–30 years experience. They get placed into companies on a fractional or interim basis — walk in, assess the tech landscape, make strategic decisions fast. Clients span public, private, and PE-backed companies across all industries.

**Key insight:** These aren't buyers. They're influencers and recommenders. When a fractional CTO lands inside a company and says "we need an implementation partner," that's pipeline. Every person in this room is a potential referral channel. They also care about looking smart to their clients — the AI maturity framework is a tool *they* can use.

---

## Talk Structure

### 1. Open with the room (30 sec)

"Everyone in this room walks into a company and has to figure out what's real and what's noise — fast. That's what we do too."

### 2. Who is Improving (60 sec)

Texas-born, $72M+ Texas revenue, founded 2007. Software development, consulting, training. Not a body shop — a firm that embeds with your team. Local to every market Fortium operates in Texas.

### 3. The AI maturity hook (90 sec)

"The number one question every CTO is getting right now is 'what's our AI strategy?' We built a framework to answer that honestly."

Hit the 8-stage model at headline level — most companies are at Stage 2 (using tools, not getting the multiplier). The real shift happens at Stage 3 when you let AI do work you're accountable for. That's where coaching matters more than tooling.

### 4. The value to them specifically (60 sec)

"When you land in a new engagement and need to assess where a company actually is with AI — not where they think they are — we built a diagnostic for that. We also have a 12-week program that moves teams from Stage 2 to Stage 4. If you're inside a company that needs that, we're the partner who delivers it."

### 5. Close clean (30 sec)

"Our team is here all day — Rene, Diana, Don, Derek, Manolo, and me. Find any of us over lunch. Enjoy the meal."

## What NOT to do

- Don't pitch services generically
- Don't list capabilities
- Don't talk about Improving's history
- Lead with the framework — it's the thing that makes you different and gives *them* a tool they can use
