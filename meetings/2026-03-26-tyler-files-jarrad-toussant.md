## Strategic Angles

### Why Say Yes
1. **Audience = decision-makers.** 50 senior HR/Talent leaders from major DFW companies. These are the people who approve consulting engagements, hire firms for transformation work, and greenlight training budgets.
2. **AI + Talent is your sweet spot.** The intersection of AI adoption and workforce transformation is exactly what Improving sells and what you talk about. This isn't a generic speaking gig — it's a curated audience of buyers.
3. **Deepens DRC relationship.** You're already on two task forces. Adding Talent Labs builds a third thread and puts you in front of a different audience segment (HR leaders vs. tech/policy leaders).
4. **Convergence AI flywheel.** If you're speaking at Convergence AI (Mar 30-31) AND Talent Labs (Q3), you become the DRC's go-to AI voice. That's a valuable position.
5. **Content already exists.** You've already done "AI for Executives" — this is an adaptation, not a build-from-scratch.

### What to Clarify on the Call
- **Session format:** Keynote? Workshop? Panel? How long?
- **Date:** "Q3" is broad — July? August? September?
- **Topic scoping:** Do they want the same "AI for Executives" content, or tailored for HR/Talent? (HR angle could be: AI's impact on hiring, upskilling, org design, workforce planning)
- **Audience composition:** What types of companies? Enterprise? Mid-market? Industry mix?
- **Co-promotion:** Will Improving be credited/promoted as a partner or just David as an individual speaker?
- **Recording/distribution:** Will the session be recorded or shared beyond the cohort?
