# Architecting the AI-Driven Business Model

---

## Episode Overview

We'll dig into what an AI-driven business model actually looks like — beyond the buzzwords and beyond simple automation. John brings a builder's perspective on what it takes to make an organization's technology stack AI-ready, from data strategy and governance to human-AI collaboration and the cultural shift required to make it stick.
