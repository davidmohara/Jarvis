### The Evolution of AI: Where It Started and Where We Are Now

## Episode Overview

We'll trace the arc of artificial intelligence — from its origins and early pioneers through the breakthroughs that turned theory into reality, all the way to today's agentic and generative AI landscape. Scott brings the rare perspective of someone who's lived the evolution firsthand, from railroad automation systems to leading Improving's ML and AI practice.

## Key Discussion Topics

| # | TOPIC | FOCUS |
|---|-------|-------|

## Prompting Questions

**1. On Origins**

> *"When we talk about the origin of AI, where does that story actually begin? And who are the early pioneers who laid the foundation?"*

**2. On Breakthroughs**

> *"What were the breakthroughs that turned the AI vision into reality? Was there a moment where you thought, 'This has real potential — I'm excited about this'?"*

**3. On Today's Reality**

> *"Today, what does AI do exceptionally well? And on the flip side — where is it still falling short? Is there anything you thought AI would be able to do by now that it can't?"*

**4. On The Big Shift**

> *"We're seeing a massive shift from using AI as a tool to using it as a collaborator. Do you think people are prepared for that? And what skills are becoming more important for humans as we make that transition?"*

**5. On What's Next**

> *"What do you think the next major breakthrough will be? And how might AI reshape entire industries and the way we do work?"*

**6. Wrap-Up**

> *"What are some of the key takeaways you would want someone to have from this discussion?"*
