Season 1, Episode 7

AI Strategies: Why You Need One for Your Business

Episode Overview

We'll dig into what it actually means to have an AI strategy — and why so many organizations get this wrong. With 60-80% of AI projects failing, the difference between success and wasted investment almost always comes down to having a clear plan. Devlin brings a C-suite lens as someone who's helped dozens of enterprises navigate this exact question — and has the scars to prove what happens when you skip the strategy.
