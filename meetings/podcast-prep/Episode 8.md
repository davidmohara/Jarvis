### AI-Ready Data: The New Benchmark of Enterprise Competitiveness

## Episode Overview

We'll dig into why data readiness — not the model you pick — is the real competitive moat in the AI era. Most enterprises think they're further along than they are, and the gap between perception and reality is where AI investments go to die. Nick brings two decades of enterprise engineering experience to the question every leader needs to answer before they spend another dollar on AI tooling.
