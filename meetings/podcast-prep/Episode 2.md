### Architecting the AI-Driven Business Model

## Episode Overview

This episode explores what it really means to build an AI-driven business model—beyond just automation. We'll discuss how AI changes value creation and delivery, the key architectural components needed, challenges like ethics and organizational resistance, and practical advice for businesses starting their AI journey.

## Key Discussion Topics

| # | TOPIC | FOCUS |
|---|-------|-------|
| 1 | **AI-Driven Foundations** | What does AI-driven mean beyond automation? How does AI change value creation and delivery? |
| 2 | **Key Components** | Data & governance strategy, cloud/APIs for scalability, human-AI collaboration. |
| 3 | **Challenges & Risks** | AI bias, transparency, accountability. Human resistance and cultural shifts needed. |
| 4 | **Future Opportunities** | Generative AI trends, customer experience transformation, skills needed for AI-first world. |
| 5 | **Practical Advice** | Guidance for businesses just getting started on their AI journey. |
