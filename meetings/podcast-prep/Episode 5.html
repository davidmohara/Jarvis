Season 1, Episode 5

Orchestrating Agents: The New Scarce Skill

Episode Overview

We'll explore what it actually means to orchestrate multiple AI agents to tackle complex tasks — and why this is quickly becoming one of the most valuable skills in enterprise technology. Robyn brings a practitioner's lens from running a large delivery organization, drawing parallels between leading teams of people and coordinating teams of AI agents.

Key Discussion Topics

# TOPIC FOCUS
1 Generative vs. Agentic AI The critical difference between generative and agentic AI, and why it matters for orchestration.
2 Multi-Agent Coordination How to coordinate multiple AI agents simultaneously to complete complex tasks — and why this is important.
3 Orchestration as Leadership How orchestration mirrors leadership — assigning ownership, designing interfaces, maintaining quality control.
4 Deployment & Architecture Centralized vs. decentralized orchestration, hybrid approaches, and choosing what's right for your org.
5 Real-World Application Use cases, industry examples, and how this is already changing the way we work.
