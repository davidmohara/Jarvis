# Responsibilities

## Regional Director — What David Owns

1. Leads One Texas as the epitome of Go-To-Market Strategy (2026)
2. Accountable to Focused Growth of multi-enterprise group: $100M to $150M (2026)
3. Initiates a relentless Culture of Revenue among Texas leadership (2026)
4. Responsible for Growth of Executive Leaders in Sales and Delivery (2027)
5. Reports to Managing Director. Texas Presidents report to David.

## What David Does

- Engages Strategic Services (Global GTM Offerings) into the region
- Creates growth programs and campaigns for designated accounts
- Designs weekly executive experiences: QBRs, one-on-ones, group activities, workshops
- Participates in weekly Sales Leadership with local sales teams and Presidents
- Leads monthly Sales Strategy with Sales VPs and Presidents
- Elevates Global Delivery strategies for Qualified Account Plans
- Coordinates corporate resources toward strategic opportunities: Partners, Recruiting, GRO, SPARC, Partner Desk
- Captains 3-year vision for specific Regional Growth

## What David Does NOT Do

- Resource operations (assignments, utilization, HR) — GRO + Presidents + MD
- Sales management or CRM discipline — SPARC + Presidents
- Client management — local AEs maintain accounts
- Writing proposals or decks for sales team
