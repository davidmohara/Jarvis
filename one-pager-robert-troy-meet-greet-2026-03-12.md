## Why He's in Dallas

This is Minister Troy's **St. Patrick's Day trade mission to Texas.** The Irish government dispatches ministers to major cities worldwide each March to strengthen business and cultural ties. Troy's Texas itinerary includes:

- **Dallas:** Meet & greet with industry leaders (tonight's event), Irish Business Network Dallas, Texas Stock Exchange visit, Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas
- **Austin/SXSW:** Irish tech and creative showcasing at South by Southwest — a major stage for Irish startups and artists

His portfolio covers **international financial services** — Ireland is a major European fintech hub (Stripe, Fenergo, etc.) and actively courts US companies to establish EU operations through Ireland. He recently led a similar trade mission to New York focused on Ireland's financial services sector.

## His Policy Priorities

- **Financial services & fintech** — Promoting Ireland as a European financial services hub. Led the New York trade mission for international financial services.
- **Credit unions** — Modernizing Ireland's credit union sector
- **Insurance reform** — Addressing cost and availability issues in Irish insurance market
- **"Right to Be Forgotten"** — Championed legislation allowing cancer survivors to access insurance/mortgages without disclosing past diagnoses (personal connection — featured in Cancerworld Magazine)
