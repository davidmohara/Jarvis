# Gold Forum Retreat - Light Connection Agenda & Exercises

**Dates:** May 6-8, 2026
**Goal:** Create comfort, deepen trust, and strengthen connection without making the retreat feel heavy or over-structured.

---

## Exercises

### 1) The Story Behind the Story
**Time:** 60-90 minutes

Each person shares:
- A story others may already know (success, failure, life event)
- Then answers: "What did no one see or understand about that at the time?"

**Purpose:** Builds depth without forcing vulnerability. Creates empathy quickly.
**Tip:** 8-10 minutes per person to keep energy high.

### 2) User Manual of Me
**Time:** 75-90 minutes

Each person shares:
- How to get the best out of me
- What stresses me out (early signs)
- How I show up under pressure
- What support helps vs. doesn't

**Purpose:** Improves how the Forum functions going forward. Practical and insightful.
**Tip:** Have people prepare notes in advance.

### 3) Proudest + Hardest (Last 12 Months)
**Time:** 90-120 minutes

Each person shares:
- 1 thing they are most proud of (and why it matters)
- 1 thing that was hardest (focus on feelings, not just facts)

**Purpose:** Balanced reflection. Encourages meaningful sharing without being too heavy.
**Tip:** Gently steer away from purely business updates.

### 4) Paired Walk & Talk
**Time:** 60-75 minutes

**Format:** Pair members up (preferably those less connected)

Prompts:
- "Something I'm currently figuring out..."
- "A belief I'm questioning..."
- "Where I feel most/least fulfilled..."

**Purpose:** Enables deeper 1:1 sharing. Keeps energy light and natural.
**Tip:** Rotate partners if time allows.

### 5) Turning Points (Lite Version)
**Time:** 90-120 minutes (2-3 people max)

Each person shares:
- 3-5 key turning points that shaped who they are
- Focus on meaning and impact, not full life history

**Purpose:** Adds depth without intensity of full lifelines. Sets up future retreats for deeper work.

### 6) Appreciations (Closing)
**Time:** 45-60 minutes

Each person shares 1-2 genuine appreciations for others.

**Purpose:** Strengthens bonds. Leaves group on a high note.
**Tip:** Be specific ("When you said X, it impacted me because...")

### 7) Future Snapshot (Optional)
**Time:** 60 minutes

Each person shares: "A year from now, I'd feel great if..."

**Focus:** Feelings, life direction, relationships (not just goals)
**Purpose:** Forward-looking alignment. Creates future Forum discussion themes.

---

## Sample 2-Day Flow

### Day 1 (May 6)
- Arrival + casual dinner
- Story Behind the Story
- Optional informal fireside time

### Day 2 (May 7)
**Morning:**
- Proudest + Hardest

**Midday:**
- Shared activity
- Paired Walk & Talk

**Afternoon:**
- Turning Points (2 people)

**Evening:**
- Relaxed social time

### Day 3 - Departure (May 8)
- User Manual of Me (shortened)
- Appreciations
- Future Snapshot (optional)

---

## Key Guardrails
- Don't overpack the schedule (leave white space)
- Keep alcohol out of Forum sessions
- Ensure equal airtime
- Recommit to confidentiality: "Nothing. Nobody. Never."
- End sessions while energy is still high

---

## Core Principle

> The goal is not maximum depth. The goal is building trust, comfort, and connection so deeper work comes naturally in future retreats.
